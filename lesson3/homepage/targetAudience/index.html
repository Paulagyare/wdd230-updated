Target Audience

Our main targeted audience are young adults with unique talents, who are likely between the ages of 16 and 25 years who can access our website on work computers, and also on their mobile devices.

Persona 1 - John

John is a 20-year-old college student who has a passion for music production. He is interested in exploring different music genres and creating his own music.

  • Scenario 1: John is interested in joining our community to connect with other music producers. He wants to know how he can benefit from a gold membership.
  • Scenario 2: John lives in a small town and wants to know how many people in his area are interested in music production.
  • Scenario 3: John is considering canceling his membership and wants to know who he can talk to about canceling.

Persona 2 - Maria

Maria is a 24-year-old graphic designer who is interested in networking with other creatives to expand her skills and knowledge.

  • Scenario 1: Maria wants to know if there are any upcoming events or workshops on graphic design that she can attend.
  • Scenario 2: Maria is considering upgrading to a gold membership and wants to know what additional benefits she will receive.
  • Scenario 3: Maria is having trouble accessing the website on her mobile device and wants to know if there is a solution.

Persona 3 - David

David is a 22-year-old aspiring entrepreneur who is interested in starting his own business. He is looking for resources and support to help him get started.

  • Scenario 1: David wants to know if there are any business mentors or advisors available through the community.
  • Scenario 2: David is interested in attending networking events to meet other young entrepreneurs. He wants to know if there are any events coming up.
  • Scenario 3: David is having trouble navigating the website and wants to know if there is a tutorial or guide available.

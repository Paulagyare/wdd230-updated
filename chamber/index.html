Our chamber of commerce is committed to promoting economic growth and success for all of our members. As a member, you will have access to a wide range of resources and networking opportunities that will help you grow your business and connect with other like-minded professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of unity and collaboration among businesses in Accra, Darkuman by providing resources, support, and advocacy to help our members achieve success. We believe that by working together, we can create a thriving business community that benefits everyone.

Membership Benefits

  • Networking opportunities with other business professionals
  • Access to exclusive business resources, including workshops and seminars
  • Advocacy and representation on key business issues
  • Promotion and marketing of your business through our website, social media channels, and events
  • Discounts on products and services from other chamber members
